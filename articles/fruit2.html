Orange juice

Fresh orange juice is a storehouse of ascorbic acid. Everyone knows about it. But this is not the only advantage of orange juice, which is worth knowing about. It can be used to promote health, give a radiant appearance, and also be used to prepare various delicious dishes.

This juice contains a large amount of vitamins C, A, E and K, macro- and microelements, antioxidant substances, flavonoids, pectins

Juice made from orange peel and pulp is considered especially beneficial for health. The peel of oranges contains more vitamins, flavonoids, organic acids, essential oils. The composition of the peel also includes an aromatic hydrocarbon - d-limonene, which has a pronounced citrus smell and has many beneficial properties for the human body. You can get such a healthy drink in a juicer or blender.
