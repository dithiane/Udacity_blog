Tomato, Celery, Potato juices

Doctors even prescribe apple-tomato fresh in the post-infarction period. It normalizes metabolism, reduces sugar levels, which means it is just a gift for diabetics. Prepare such a mix in a 2:1 ratio (2 parts tomato and 1 part apples) and in no case do not add salt! Salt in this combination, although it will make the taste more "familiar", but neutralizes many useful substances.

Fresh celery is a complex protection of our body. A glass of celery juice, drunk in the morning on an empty stomach, strengthens the walls of blood vessels, normalizes blood pressure, prevents gastritis and varicose veins, mercilessly fights against any inflammatory processes in the body!

An unexpected participant in our hit parade is fresh potato. Of course, it does not taste very familiar, but it perfectly strengthens the immune system and improves the functioning of the organs of the gastrointestinal tract. It relieves pain, has an antimicrobial effect, normalizes blood sugar. Potato juice is also very rich in antioxidants, according to Researchgate.
