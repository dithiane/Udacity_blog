Carrot juice

Carrot juice is a mix of beauty and health! Carrots are low in calories and high in vitamin A, biotin, and potassium, according to the American health publication Healthline. Vitamin A is responsible for the condition of our hair, nails, improves skin condition, strengthens immunity and vision.

Carrots pair well with lemon, celery, beetroot, pumpkin, apple, and orange.

During the period of seasonal colds, juice from carrots and celery will help if you mix them in equal proportions. You can add some spinach and parsley to this mix.

Carrot-pumpkin juice perfectly strengthens the heart and blood vessels, also in a 1:1 ratio. But both of these vegetables contain a lot of carotene, so it is better to dilute such fresh juice with boiled water and drink no more than 0.5 cups.
